# Fruit Fight — Ruleset

> Keep this file in sync with the How to Play page in `Fruit-Fight.html` whenever rules or mechanics change.

---

## Goal

Match identical fruits along win lines to score points. You have **15 spins** and **3 minutes**.

---

## The Reels

- 5 reels, 3 visible symbols each (top and bottom symbols dimmed as peek)
- **5 win lines:**

| Line | Pattern |
|---|---|
| 1 | Centre row across all 5 reels |
| 2 | Top row across all 5 reels |
| 3 | Bottom row across all 5 reels |
| 4 | Top → Centre → Bottom → Centre → Top |
| 5 | Bottom → Centre → Top → Centre → Bottom |

Lines pay **both ways** — left-to-right and right-to-left.

---

## Actions

| Action | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Spin** | 1 spin | Rotates all non-held reels to a new outcome |
| **Hold** | 1 hold (max 8) | Locks a reel — its symbol carries over to the next spin |
| **Nudge** | 1 nudge (max 8) | Shifts one reel up or down by one symbol — free to use, no spin consumed |

---

## Symbols & Paytable

Rarer symbols pay more. Cherry is the only symbol that pays on 2 of a kind.

| Symbol | 2x | 3x | 4x | 5x |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cherry | 1 | 1 | 1.25 | 5.25 |
| Orange | — | 2.5 | 12.5 | 62.5 |
| Grape | — | 5 | 25 | 125 |
| Watermelon | — | 10 | 50 | 250 |
| Seven | — | 50 | 250 | 1250 |

Payouts apply per line. Both LTR and RTL wins on the same line count separately — unless they share the same symbols (no double-counting).

---

## Game End

The game ends when:
- All 15 spins are used
- The 3-minute timer hits 0:00

Final score is saved to the leaderboard.
