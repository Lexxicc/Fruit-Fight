How to Play

The Basics

Match identical fruits along 5 lines. Lines pay both ways — left-to-right and right-to-left.

Actions

• Spin — costs 1 spin. Timer starts on your first spin.

• Hold — lock a reel before the next spin. Costs 1 Hold.

• Nudge — shift a reel up or down one symbol. Free, limited supply.

Ending the Game

• Run out of spins, or let the 3-minute timer hit zero.

• Your final score is saved to High Scores.
